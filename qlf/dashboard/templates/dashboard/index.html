Welcome to DESI Quick Look!

DESI Quick Look is being developed for monitoring the DESI observations in real time.

  • QA summarizes the QA results at the exposure level and link to QA results for individual cameras.
  • Observing History shows time series and statistics for the current or for a range of nights.
  • QL Pipeline follows the progress of the data reduction and control the Quick Look pipeline execution.
