Welcome to DESI Quick Look!

DESI Quick Look is used to monitor the DESI observations in real time.

  • Exposures summarize QA results at the exposure level and link to QA results for individual cameras.
  • Night summary shows time series plots and statistics for the observing night.
  • Pipeline can be used to follow the progress of the data reduction, control the pipeline execution and access the pipeline logs.
