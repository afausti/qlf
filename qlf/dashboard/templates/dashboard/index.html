Welcome to DESI Quick Look!

DESI Quick Look is used to monitor the DESI observations in real time.

  • QA monitor summarizes the QA results at the exposure level and link to QA results for individual cameras.
  • Night summary shows time series and statistics for the current night.
  • Observing History shows time series and statistics for multiple nights.
  • Pipeline Execution follows the progress of the data reduction and control the Quick Look pipeline execution.
